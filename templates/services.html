Services

  • SAT and ACT Preparation

    We’ll discuss your goals and whether the ACT or SAT would be a better fit. Each week, you’ll learn curriculum reflecting your needs to answer real test questions in class and homework. You’ll take actual, timed tests to track your progress and build experience. As you apply valuable concepts and test-taking tips, you’ll gain higher scores and life-long learning skills to get you into a college you want and beyond. The number of sessions is flexible with many students preparing for weekly sessions in one or more cycles of 8 weeks.

  • Subject-Matter Tests

    We use a similar approach based on my experience in preparing students for subject-matter tests in ELA, History and related Social Studies subjects. As a certified teacher, I’ve taught History Regents classes and am certified to teach AP US History. No matter where you start, I can help you understand key questions, concepts, and processes to do your best in subject matter tests.

  • Essays and Other Writing Assignments

    As an editor, parent, and teacher of middle and high school students, I enjoy helping writers develop their thoughts and voice, including in compelling application essays. In nurturing brainstorming, planning, drafting, and revising exercises, you’ll develop your creative powers of narration and persuasion.

  • Homework and Subject Tutoring

    Drawing on over twenty years as an award-winning editor, teacher, and tutor, I’ll help you make the most of your school and professional assignments. I have in-depth content knowledge and experience mentoring beginners to acclaimed scholars. I have also worked closely with students with special needs and English language learners. Powerful reading and writing strategies will deepen your understanding and pleasure in learning. You’ll hone your research, reflection, and writing skills in the following areas among others: Language Arts, US and World History, Geography, Participation in Government, Economics, Psychology, Criminal Justice, Research and Citation, and Study Skills.
